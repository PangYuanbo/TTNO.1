Our Mission and Goals

Mission: Table Tennis No. 1 is committed to providing professional training, quality facilities and community support for table tennis enthusiasts, to inspire each member's passion for table tennis, to develop skills and to promote the appeal of the sport.

Vision: To be the most influential and engaging table tennis club in the region, creating a vibrant, innovative and fun community for table tennis enthusiasts of all ages through excellence in service and diversity of activities.

Goals:

  • Develop skills: Provide personalized training programs for members of all levels to help them continuously improve their skills.
  • Promote the sport: Attract more people to learn about and join the sport of table tennis by organizing open tournaments, friendly matches and community events.
  • Promote multiculturalism: Promote table tennis in a multicultural context, facilitating intercultural exchange and integration, so that more people can experience the appeal of the sport.
  • Support women's table tennis: Encourage more women to participate in table tennis through dedicated training programs, tournaments and activities to promote gender equality and popularity of the sport.
  • Enhance the community: Create a supportive and socially friendly environment where members can not only enjoy the sport, but also make meaningful connections.
  • Facility Optimization: Continuously improve club venues and equipment to provide members with the best possible training and playing experience.
  • Environmental Protection and Health: Promote green concepts and healthy lifestyles to improve members' physical and mental health through sports.
